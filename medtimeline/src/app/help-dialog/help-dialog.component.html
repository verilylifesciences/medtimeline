MedTimeLine intro screen
Change the dates of the data displayed on the chart using datepickers.
Click on the datepicker icon to open a calendar.
The Timeline feature allows you to enter custom events.
Click anywhere on the timeline to add an event.
By default, descriptions for events are hidden. Click on the expander to reveal the description.
Click on the pencil icon to edit the event.
Click on the trashcan icon to delete the event.
For charts with legends, hover over a series to highlight that series on the chart. Click on a series to hide it from the chart.
The green highlighted area on some charts represents a "normal range" indicated in the standard.
The card container displays all charts selected.
Click and drag a chart to rearrange on the page.
Click on the trashcan icon to remove a card from the page. Press "Undo" at the bottom of the page to restore.
You can add cards to the top of the page using the toolbar.
The "Save Snapshot" button allows you to save a static image of the current page to the patient record.
