MedTimeLine Configuration Screen
Click on the current date range to open the datepicker.
Choose a patient encounter or pre-set time period on the left.
Alternatively, choose a custom date range.
The Custom Timeline feature allows you to enter custom events.
Click the pencil icon to enter edit mode.
Click the timeline at a place where you would like to add a flag.
In the dialog box, enter a title, description, and select a color for the flag.
You can also manually adjust the date and/or time.
While in edit mode, you can edit an existing flag, which will open up the dialog box again.
You can also remove flags while in edit mode.
You can enter notes in the main timeline display area.
Click the pencil icon to enter edit mode.
Type in your notes, and click the save button to save the note.
You can add, remove, and rearrange timelines to optimize the data view for specific patients.
To add a timeline, click the plus sign, then select Add Data Timeline. Select the timeline type you want to add, then select the specific timeline.
You can also add another Custom Timeline or text notes. You can also search for concept through the autocomplete input.
To remove a timeline, hover your cursor over the timeline you want to remove. Click the X in the upper left corner of the timeline.
You can undo a deletion of a card, as well.
To rearrange timelines, click the label or colored handle on the left hand side of the timeline you want to move, and hold the mouse button and drag and drop the timeline to your desired location.
Timelines are categorized and color-coded by a vertical bar on the leftmost side of the data timeline.
You can hover over plotted data point and additional data displays in a tooltip.
